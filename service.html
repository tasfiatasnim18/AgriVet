FAQ

Common Pet Health Problems and Expert Solutions: Your Questions Answered

Q:What should I do if my dog has an upset stomach?

A: If your dog has an upset stomach, withhold food for 12-24 hours, then reintroduce bland food. If symptoms persist, consult your vet.

Q: Why is my cat constantly scratching?

A: Excessive scratching in cats can be due to fleas, allergies, or dry skin. Check for fleas and consider a vet visit for proper treatment.

Q: How can I treat my rabbit’s dental problems?

A: Rabbits need constant access to hay to keep their teeth trimmed. If your rabbit has dental issues, visit a vet for proper care and advice.

Q: Why is my bird pulling out its feathers?

A: Feather plucking in birds can be caused by stress, boredom, or health issues. Provide environmental enrichment and consult a vet for a full check-up.

Q: What should I do if my dog is limping?

A: Limping can be due to injury or arthritis. Limit activity and consult a vet to determine the cause and best treatment.

Q: How can I help my cat with urinary issues?

A: Cats with urinary problems may need a special diet or medication. If your cat is urinating outside the litter box or showing discomfort, see a vet right away.
